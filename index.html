The four guarantees

Every Magen agent ships with all four.

This is what “provable safety” means. Not vibes. Receipts.

Policy Gate

Every action checked against deterministic Python policies before it runs. No LLMs in the safety layer.

Signed Receipt

Cryptographic proof of every action — what happened, who requested it, what policy allowed it.

Human Gate

High-risk ops pause and email a human for approval. Signed link. One-time use. Auto-deny on timeout.

One-Click Undo

Pre-action state capture means rollback is one command. Rows restored, branches deleted, PRs closed.
